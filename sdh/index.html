About Us

Established in 2009

Salem Dhawi Electromechanical Establishment (SDH) is a wholly owned private Company offering contracting services, particularly in the public and private sectors throughout the UAE. SDH can provide a fully combined Electrical and Mechanical service in which clients benefit from a seamless supply covering all their M&E requirements. SDH is located in Mussafah sanyya M-17 area, Abu Dhabi having 25 fulltime technical Personnel and is proficient in all aspects of Electrical and maintenance with extensive experience in commercial and industrial installations. SDH has substantial experience with all facets of Electrical contracting and has successfully completed our projects. Since business was established, we have worked with many leading Clients, Consultants, contractors and suppliers.  Entering to the fifth year we look forward for many expansions in field of contracting and maintenance.
