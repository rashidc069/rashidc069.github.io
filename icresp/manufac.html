Manufacturing ERP

iCresp for manufacturing includes major modules like Manufacturing, Sales, Purchase, Inventory Management, Finanace management and reports.

Key Benefits:

> Increase efficiency of manufacturing processes
> Minimize unnecessary production downtime
> Deliver customer orders ontime
> Increase customer satisfaction
> Combine multiple orders to single invoice.
> Barcoding for pieces and cases.
> Various reports - accounting reports, vat/gst reports, sales reports, purchase reports, stock reports etc.


Delivery Plan

Easy interface for planning goods delivery for customers. System shall give information on which all orders can be closed based on current stock. System suggests matching case numbers based on customer orders. Just select the case numbers available for each orders and click 'Delivery'. This will help business to make delivery and dispatch process efficiently.

Production Plan

Interface for Production manager to decide each day’s productions planning based on customer orders and current stock.System shows product wise requirements and easily plan daily productions based on customer orders. Production and Order dispatch can be linked to avoid excess stock in warehouse.

Production

Production has 2 phases

1. Semi-Finished Goods Manufacturing Interface for tracking raw material input and Semi-product output.

2. Finished Goods Manufacturing Interface for tracking semi-product input and finished goods output.

Corresponding stocks of raw materials, semi-finished and finished goods will be managed accurately.
