Construction ERP

Web based construction management ERP suitable for Planning, Execution and finanacial management of Construction works.Major Modules: Estimation & Planning, Manage Construction Works, Administration & Financial management

Estimation & Planning:

  • Option for Setting estimation rules for Materials and Labor works.
  • Manage customer site information
  • Classify projects as Labor contract and full contract
  • Options to store and view latest material prices
  • Category wise estimation facility.
  • Work confirmation after adding required profit margin.
  • Detailed estimation print for customer

Manage Construction Works:

  • Daily Labor Assignment for each sites
  • Track labor wages with respect to their duty type (Half Day, Full Day, Over Time)
  • Track incoming raw materials in each sites with supplier,vehicle, cost, expenses etc.
  • Options to add category wise daily expenses in each sites.
  • Options to add additionanl charges to Customer.

Administration & Financial management

  • Estimation approval
  • Manage labors with contact information and wages (Half Day, Full Day, Over Time)
  • Manage labor payments
  • Manage customer receipts
  • Manage site wise supplier payments
  • Manage site wise expense payments
  • Manage vehicle payments
  • Manage Cash/Bank/Cheques
  • Reports to view all activities in each sites.
