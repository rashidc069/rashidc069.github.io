Retail / Wholesale

VAT / GST enabled Cloud ERP solution with rich set of user friendly functionalities for managing the business processes. System includes Financial Accounting, Sales, Procurement and Inventory Management modules. This accounting and inventory ERP software system is highly customised for retail-wholesale industry.

Modules

  • Sale
  • Procurement
  • Inventory
  • Godown Management System
  • Finance & Accounting
  • User management

  • Warranty & Service Management

  • Freight management

Main Features

  • VAT/GST enabled
  • Barcode facility available
  • Manage executives
  • Manage branches
  • Centralised Purchase Control
  • Single/Multi user environment
  • Easy access to category wise stock reports
  • Manage multiple warehouses,damaged stocks, Sample stock etc.
  • System generated purchase order (Wish list)
  • Purchase order and Purchase delivery option
  • Sale quotation facility

  • Credit limit facility
